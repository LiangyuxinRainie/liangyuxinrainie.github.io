I’m Yuxin Liang(梁雨欣), a second-year master student from School of [EIC](https://ei.hust.edu.cn/), [Huazhong University of Science and Technology](https://www.hust.edu.cn/), China, advised by [Prof. Peng Yang](https://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=en&user=8v7c4rYAAAAJ). I received my B.Eng. degree at Wuhan University of Technology, China. My current research interest includes Networking for AI 💻, Mobile Edge Computing and Edge Intelligence 📱, Mobile AIGC Networks 🌟, Distributed Inference 📚, Diffusion Models and Generative AI 🖼️.

My email: yuxinliang@hust.edu.cn; yuxinliang155@gmail.com
